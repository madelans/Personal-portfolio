Who Am I

I am passionate about design and project, I studied architecture for four years and I am currently developing my career as a Frontend Developer. I love designing and doing projects. I combine my architectural skills such as strategic analysis in project development and creative logic to my current study of software development. How do I relate my passions? "Architecture is the set of techniques that project, design, build and modify the user's habitat" for me in programming this concept is similar to the difference that its habitat would be the software and the structure is the code. For the rest, it is necessary to have logical and creative capacity, analysis of the problem and the client's descriptive memory and elaboration of potential design solutions through a process of hypothesis, evaluation and reevaluation. I synthesize ideas through diagrams and diagrams, clearly expressing ideas and solutions. And lastly, I design the code.
